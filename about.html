What We Offer

Consulting

A consultant is the one who follows construction projects and keeps his eyes open for anything that may be worth looking at that another may not have a clue about. Did you know, for example, that if someone were to be injured in the workplace (say your building), it is you who bears the responsibility if safety has not been ensured. We keep track of such details so that you do not get into trouble if something should happen, and we then also make sure that, like the example above, accidents do not happen by following our protocols that were designed to create a good working environment. However, this is only a small part of what a consult does, the person also makes sure that all the paperwork that must be signed is available, that the construction goes according to the standard that is said and must not be cheated with. The list can be longer, so you can generally say that the construction consultant is the one who manages your project and leads it to a result with a good standard.


Guidance

When it comes to building, there are usually, if not always, many questions that come up. We are well aware of this and do everything in our power to ensure that these questions can be answered as quickly and clearly as possible. Sometimes the questions are about more than what the individual himself can do and then it is great to have us by his side, because we have a part within the municipality that makes what requires more out of the project such as paper production and so on is us close at hand. In this way, our guidance makes your journey in the project more smooth and perhaps even more relaxed because we make sure that what needs to be done and provided is always in place. With us, there is always the opportunity to contact us on various forums such as email, phone, but you can also book a meeting with us by sending an email with this request. Regardless, we also make sure that three meetings take place. This to make sure everything is right before, during and after the project.


Final

In the final step, we offer you an inspection that ensures that the project is approved, has no possibility of water damage, is handicapped accessible, straight floor without a boom (which means that the floor is not uneven and can create future damage to the building), has all papers available and with an endorsement so that it can then be handed in to the municipality and finally lead to the key ending up in your hand. Of course, there are more points in this that can be underlined, but for the different projects, different points may apply. Thus, as we only mention a few general ones here to give a clear picture of what a final description actually offers and does. Above all, it ensures that the project is not left without everything being ready and ready. In this way, customers will continue to save money in the future by reducing the cost of repairs, which in the long run may grow large. Therefore, we offer this final stage so that the customer can be proud and feel good in their construction for a long time to come.
