About Us



The Company


We would like to call ourselves your construction consulting company, created so that you and your construction projects can have someone to trust and feel safe with. This with the knowledge that our service will guide during the process, ensure that everything is done safely, that the construction work environment remains safe and according to standard and that your project will end with an approved result inspected by professionals who have worked in the profession for a long time.


Our Team

John Doe

A always bright sided man with lots of knowledge in the construction field that he happily share with each and everyone. John has been working with different building services but entered our company after finding the ambition to guide and help other individuals i their building journey. He always has his phone close and makes sure that every question the customer might have are answered within a short amount of time.

Nina Andersson

This serious, ambitious and brightsided woman also known as the founder of the company. Keeps here attention on giving service that makes customers enjoy their construction journey by providing as much information and knowledge possible to everyone involved.

Our Vision

Our company provide services that inform, create security and provide a result that all parties can feel satisfied with. Our ambition is to be present in as many places as possible and despite the fact that we are still a fairly small company, we work hard to achieve that goal. This also means that we want to ensure that the company stays up to date and constantly develops, hence we are also careful to work so that the environment is more in our development plan and that the development in it stays with us as it does with other things.

The purpose of this is to stick to environmental considerations even though we are allowed to drive a lot to the various places in Sweden where our job and customers are. "Safety with thought from the inside out, prevention with the help of good planning, structure and a lot of knowledge." That's is how we look at our vision.
